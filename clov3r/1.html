Solradic, 19th Carpeon, 1

hiya!! this world doesn't have tumblr as far as i can tell but i figured out i can write my thoughts out and it manifests physically??? as a blog post??? (´･ω･`)? idk how it works but i guess i get to have a blog now!

though i'm not fully sure what to use a blog for. maybe to talk about my week ㄟ( ▔, ▔ )ㄏ? a couple interesting things happened. i've been going on expeditions with the explorer's guild the past few weeks, and this week's one was pretty funny (if a little bit silly ( •̀ ω •́ )✧), an expedition posted by a guy called lance agreste

me and 6 other people show up and we meet lance and who i can only assume was lance's carer? i didn't ask their relation, maybe they said and i forgot (´。＿。｀). lance wants to join the "philip phan club" (i only know philip from that one time he jumped off a cliff so i'm not sure why he has a fanclub ㄟ( ▔, ▔ )ㄏ) and apparently someone told him that to join he needs to make his way to "the rock of ultimate power", a random rock in an alcove 20ish kilometres out. and basically we're told that our job is to accompany (babysit) lance on his trip

now clearly someone told him to do this bc they didn't want him around and they assumed he wouldn't do it, but i didn't want to break it to him bc honestly seeing him hyped up is rly funny o((>ω< ))o he's like if u put a 5 year old in a 20 year old's body (don't tell him i said that (⊙_⊙;))

anyway we head off. lance brings a big sword that he clearly doesn't know how to wield, one of the other guys convinces him to put it away and he starts holding his fists up like he's in a fighting game instead

the trek to the rock isn't that eventful. we see a couple giant spiders (like minecraft size spiders), so of course someone's response is to attack them immediately without checking if they're dangerous first (≧ ﹏ ≦). me and someone else has to hold lance back so he doesn't attack them with his bare fists. the spiders get killed basically instantly. i feel sorry for them (；′⌒`)

we then run into a few giant boars. this time we aren't able to stop lance as he immediately runs to one and starts punching it, which makes them aggressive bc of course i haven't made enough animal enemies by now (#｀-_ゝ-). this fight takes a bit longer, someone gets knocked out and i think someone briefly tries to drown one of the boars ??? (´･ω･`)? but eventually they die and we can keep walking

we finally get to "the rock of ultimate power". it just looks like a normal rock at first but it turns out there's a big gelatinous cube on there. i'm not sure how i didn't see it (am i stupid? (￣_￣|||)) but apparently lance didn't either. so someone has to go and make sure lance doesn't touch the cube. i think the guy silenced lance and turned into a snake and wrapped around him??? idk i was busy with the cube (￣┰￣*)

anyway we kill the cube. and before lance touches the rock, someone points out that the cube appears to have been there basically since we all got warped here, and that no one else could have touched this rock. and he breaks it to lance that whoever sent him to do this did not like him. i think he called it "hazing" but i've never heard that term before ㄟ( ▔, ▔ )ㄏ. and instead of getting upset, lance gets rly excited as he decides that bc he came to the rock, and no one else did, he must be better than the rest of the fanclub, and that must make him the president! some of the others try to dissuade him, but i encourage him bc how could i not ヾ(≧▽≦*)o

not much happens after that. we walk back to sorrowstar, someone kicks lance across the street ??? (´･ω･`)? and we get our rewards. i honestly hope i get to see lance on an expedition again it was rly fun ~(￣▽￣)~*

ok so for the next thing that happened this week. for context, i've figured out this world seems to work on the rules of a game i played back in my old world called "dungeons and dragons". if u don't know what that is idk how to explain it <(＿　＿)>. so around about when i first got here, i don't remember the exact sequence of events but at some point i became a warlock of starlight (☆▽☆). now i've never played a warlock before but i've heard it's cool so i thought i'd give it a shot o((>ω< ))o

or so i thought (#｀-_ゝ-)

cut to this week, a couple days after the expedition. a letter spawns in front of me, with 3 electrum (classic starlight (✿◡‿◡)) and a message, that basically says i was a witch the whole time and i've only now just been made a warlock??? (´･ω･`)? in hindsight i probably should have expected something like this from the goddess of tricks but rly??? (* ￣︿￣) i would not be surprised if i'm still not a warlock and i've just been made a different class i've never played but i guess we will see

anyway i think that's everything i wanted to talk about for now. if there's anything else i'll make another blog post, but who knows how long it'll be until i do that ㄟ( ▔, ▔ )ㄏ. whatever the case, seeya!
